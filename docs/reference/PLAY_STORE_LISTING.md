## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**Pontifex Industries — the bridge to your company's digital infrastructure.**

Pontifex gives growing companies one platform to run their operations and build the custom digital tools their business actually needs. Connect your office with your team in the field, replace scattered spreadsheets and paper, and put scheduling, time, work, and billing in one place that works the way you do.

**SCHEDULE & DISPATCH IN SECONDS**
• Drag-and-drop schedule board — assign your team to jobs in one move
• See every job live — who's en route, who's on site, who's done — in real time
• Send the right people to the right place with all the details attached

**ACCURATE TIME, ZERO PAPER**
• GPS clock-in/out — real timecards tied to the job location, no buddy-punching
• Attendance, overtime, and time-off in one clean view
• Approve hours in minutes instead of chasing timesheets

**DOCUMENT EVERY JOB**
• Digital work orders and daily logs from start to sign-off
• Capture production data, costs, and notes per job
• Jobsite photos straight from your team's phone

**KEEP EQUIPMENT & ASSETS RUNNING**
• Report an issue in one tap from the field
• Every request lands in the right inbox automatically
• Nothing gets lost between the field and the back office

**GET PAID FASTER**
• Turn a completed job into an invoice in one tap
• Track what's billed, sent, and paid

**BUILT YOUR WAY**
• Mobile-first and fast — designed for phones in the field
• Your branding, your workflows — a platform that adapts to your business
• Role-based access from team member to owner
• Secure and Face ID / Touch ID sign-in

Pontifex is the bridge between how your business runs today and the digital infrastructure it needs to scale. One platform. Built for your operation.

Learn more at pontifexindustries.com
