## üéØ Document Selection Tips

### For Core Drilling Jobs:
- JSA Form
- Silica Dust Control Plan
- Equipment Daily Inspection
- Pre-Work Site Inspection
- Quality Control Checklist
- Time Card
- Customer Sign-Off

### For Wall Sawing/Cutting Jobs:
- JSA Form
- Silica Dust Control Plan
- Hot Work Permit (if applicable)
- Pre-Work Site Inspection
- Equipment Daily Inspection
- Quality Control Checklist
- Time Card
- Customer Sign-Off

### For Demolition Jobs:
- JSA Form
- Site-Specific Safety Plan
- Silica Dust Control Plan
- Pre-Work Site Inspection
- Daily Production Report
- Final Job Inspection
- Time Card
- Customer Sign-Off

### Minimum Required for All Jobs:
- JSA Form (Job Safety Analysis)
- Time Card
- Customer Sign-Off
